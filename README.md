### THE MANIFESTO
**forge.** is a deterministic identity lab. It does not ask how you feel; it tells you who you are. By synthesizing live biometric data and digital consumption, it generates a **vibe receipt**—a timestamped proof of your current state.

### THE RULES
* **NO STORAGE:** The app remembers nothing. Your data is forged, printed, and purged.
* **NO OPTIONS:** You cannot mute the pulse. You cannot change the color. The truth is not customizable.
* **SHARE TO SAVE:** If you do not witness the moment by sharing or saving the receipt, it is lost to the void.

---

### THE ANATOMY OF A RECEIPT
Each scan lasts **7 seconds** and utilizes:
1.  **BIO:** Live BPM + HRV (Heart Rate Variability).
2.  **MOTION:** Step count + Gait symmetry (Last 120m).
3.  **DIGITAL:** Screen time category distribution + Pickup frequency.
4.  **ENV:** Ambient light (Lux) + Noise floor (dB).
