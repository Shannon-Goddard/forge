.forge // PRIVACY_PROTOCOL_v1.0

01_DATA_COLLECTION

We access HealthKit (iOS) and Google Fit (Android) to retrieve Heart Rate Variability (HRV) and motion data. This data is used solely to generate your biometric "Living Signal."

02_STORAGE_ZERO

Biometric data is processed in volatile memory and is never stored on any server. We do not own a database of your heart. All data stays on-device.

03_THIRD_PARTIES

We do not sell, share, or monetize user data. Our revenue is derived solely from app purchases and subscriptions.

04_RETENTION

Data is purged as soon as the biometric "Forge" is complete. Your Bio-Hash is a deterministic signature, not a record.
